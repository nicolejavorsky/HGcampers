A Sustainable Summer

Harlem Grown is on a mission to inspire youth to live HEALTHY and AMBITIOUS lives through mentorship and hands-on education in urban farming, sustainability and nutrition. We started a summer camp for youth to farm, learn, and have fun! One of our activities this summer is teaching our campers how to code. Our campers are creating websites about food production and sustainability!
